How to Join
Previous Next

Knowledge bowl is a trivia club that competes in tournaments around Memphis held in different high schools. We also compete in the TV city-wide competition. We practice on Wednesday for about an hour. There is no preparation, or experience needed to join. We encourage members to read, watch or listen to the news to study current events.
